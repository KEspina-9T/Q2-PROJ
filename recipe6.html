Biko

Ingredients

  • 2 cups glutinous rice (sweet rice)
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp butter (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the glutinous rice until the water runs clear, then cook it in a rice cooker or on the stove.
  2. In a pan, combine coconut milk, brown sugar, and salt. Cook over medium heat until the sugar dissolves and the mixture thickens (about 10 minutes).
  3. Once the rice is cooked, add it to the coconut mixture and stir well until fully combined and thick.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a greased dish, flatten it with a spatula, and let it cool.
  5. Optionally, top with butter for added richness. Serve and enjoy!
