Menudo

Ingredients

  • 500g pork, cut into small cubes
  • 1/2 cup liver (optional), cut into small cubes
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 carrot, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 cup green peas (optional)
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, then saut√© onion and garlic until softened.
  2. Add pork and liver, cook until browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce, soy sauce, and water, then simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Stir in potatoes, carrots, pepper, and green peas. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  5. Serve hot with rice. Enjoy!
