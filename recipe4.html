Beef Tapa

Ingredients

  • 500g beef (sirloin or tenderloin), thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Combine soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, sugar, pepper, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add sliced beef and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and fry marinated beef until cooked and slightly crispy.
  4. Serve with garlic rice and a fried egg. Enjoy!
