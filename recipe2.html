Ginataang Kalabasa

Ingredients

  • 1 small kalabasa (squash), peeled and cut into cubes
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce (or salt)
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 cup string beans (sitaw), cut into 2-inch pieces
  • Optional: shrimp or pork

Instructions

  1. Caramel: In a pan, melt 1 cup sugar and 2 tbsp water over low heat until golden. Pour into a flan mold.
  2. Custard: Mix 6 egg yolks, 1/2 cup sugar, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla. Strain the mixture to remove lumps.
  3. Steam: Pour custard into the mold. Steam for 40-50 minutes until set.
  4. Cool: Let it cool, then refrigerate for 2-3 hours.
  5. Serve: Invert onto a plate and enjoy!
