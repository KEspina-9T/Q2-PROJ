Sinigang

Ingredients

  • 500g pork (or shrimp, fish), cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1-2 long green chili peppers
  • 1 pack sinigang mix or 1/4 cup tamarind paste
  • 1-2 cups water (adjust for desired broth amount)
  • 1-2 cups assorted vegetables (e.g., kangkong, radish, eggplant, string beans)
  • Fish sauce or salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In a pot, combine pork, onion, tomatoes, and water. Bring to a boil and simmer until meat is tender (about 40 minutes for pork).
  2. Add the sinigang mix or tamarind paste, green chili peppers, and season with fish sauce or salt.
  3. Add the vegetables and cook for another 5-10 minutes until tender.
  4. Adjust seasoning if needed, then serve hot with rice. Enjoy!
