Adobo

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/2 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1/4 cup water (optional)

Instructions

  1. Combine soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns in a bowl. Add chicken and marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add marinated chicken, and cook until browned.
  3. Pour in the marinade and water, bring to a boil, then simmer for 25-30 minutes until tender.
  4. Serve with rice. Enjoy!
