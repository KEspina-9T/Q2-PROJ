Tinola

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
  • 1 green papaya, peeled and sliced
  • 1 bunch spinach or malunggay leaves
  • 1-2 long green chili peppers
  • 5 cups water
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a pot, saut√© onion and ginger in oil until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken and cook until browned.
  3. Pour in water, then bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes until chicken is tender.
  4. Add papaya and chili peppers, then cook for 10 more minutes until papaya is soft.
  5. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper.
  6. Stir in spinach or malunggay leaves and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with rice. Enjoy!
