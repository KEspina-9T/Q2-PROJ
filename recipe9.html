Puto

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix rice flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together coconut milk, water, egg, and vanilla extract.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients, then pour the batter into small, greased molds.
  4. Steam the molds over simmering water for about 15-20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  5. Let cool, then serve. Enjoy your puto!
