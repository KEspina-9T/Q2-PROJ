Bistek

Ingredients

  • 500g beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup calamansi juice or lemon juice
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1/4 cup water

Instructions

  1. Marinate the beef in soy sauce, calamansi juice, garlic, and pepper for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until softened, then set aside.
  3. In the same pan, cook marinated beef until browned.
  4. Add water and simmer for about 10 minutes until the beef is tender.
  5. Top with cooked onions and serve with rice. Enjoy!
