Longsilog

Ingredients

  • 4 pieces longganisa (Filipino sausage)
  • 2 cups cooked white rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the longganisa in a pan over medium heat until browned and cooked through, about 10-15 minutes.
  2. While the longganisa cooks, fry the eggs to your desired doneness (sunny-side-up or scrambled)season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Assemble the plate with cooked rice, longganisa, and eggs. Serve and enjoy!
